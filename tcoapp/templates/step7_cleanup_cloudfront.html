Delete the CloudFront Distribution

Before you delete a CloudFront distribution, you must disable it. A disabled distribution is no longer functional and does not accrue charges. You can enable a disabled distribution at any time. After you delete a disabled distribution, it is no longer available.

To disable the CloudFront distribution

  1. Open the CloudFront console .

  2. Open the context (right-click) menu for the distribution that you want to disable, and then choose Disable.

  3. When prompted for confirmation, choose Yes, Disable.

To delete a CloudFront distribution

  1. Continuing from the previous procedure, open the context (right-click) menu for a disabled distribution, and then choose Delete.

  2. When prompted for confirmation, choose Yes, Delete.
